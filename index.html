Work

Barclays Product Lab

Software Developer• october 2015-january 2016

It was an intensive program with Barclays Bank Of Kenya. The bank paired the developers in a teams of 2 and they gave each team a different problem statement. In my team we were to work with investment clubs. We did user research, design prototypes and build a solution obeying agile development principles. We later pitched to CEO, Jeremy Awori.

Tech 4 Tax Justice, Oxfam Hackathon.

Software Developer • 2 & 3 February 2016

The hackathon was about tax justice in nairobi, wajir and Turkana counties. My team focused on Turkana market traders, we came up with a solution that digitized the tax collection process and got the citizens more involved and aware about the county budget, tax allocation and expenditure. WE WERE 2ND RUNNNERS UP.

#SmartcitiesNairobi,ANGELHACK

Software Developer • 29 & 30 April 2016

The hackathon was about using Fabric twitter api to create a digital solution that will improve the nairobi city life. My team focused on the county goverment officials, we came up with a great idea that will pull data from twitter to advertise county job openings and budget and expenditure, that way reducing unemployment.
